About Indigenous Art Atlas

Community-driven, non-commercial, culturally respectful.

Mission

We help communities document and learn about Indigenous art across Australia — from ancient rock art to contemporary public works. All content is for education and cultural appreciation.

Usage Guidelines

  • Always respect Country, Traditional Owners and custodians.
  • Do not visit restricted or culturally sensitive places.
  • Do not reuse images for commercial purposes.
  • Seek permission for contemporary artworks when required.

Ethical Considerations & Disclaimers

Some entries are intentionally generalised on maps to protect sensitive locations. Information may be moderated by administrators before public display.
